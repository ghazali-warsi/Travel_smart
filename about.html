About us

An ambulance is an emergency vehicle equipped to provide immediate medical care and transport patients to healthcare facilities

eAmbulance

An ambulance is a specialized vehicle designed for transporting sick or injured individuals to medical facilities for emergency care. It is equipped with life-saving tools and staffed by trained medical personnel, such as paramedics or EMTs, who provide immediate medical assistance during transport. Ambulances often have sirens and flashing lights to alert traffic and ensure a swift, safe passage through busy roads. Their timely arrival can be critical in saving lives.
