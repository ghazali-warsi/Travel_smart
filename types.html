ICU Ambulance

An ICU (Intensive Care Unit) ambulance service is a specialized medical transport that ensures critical care for patients requiring continuous monitoring and life support. Equipped with advanced medical equipment, trained healthcare professionals, and life-saving interventions, ICU ambulances are designed to transfer patients in unstable conditions between healthcare facilities or respond to emergencies. These ambulances facilitate the transport of individuals with severe injuries, critical illnesses, or those requiring specialized medical attention. With features like ventilators, cardiac monitors, and emergency medications, ICU ambulances play a crucial role in maintaining vital functions and providing immediate medical care during transit to enhance patient outcomes.
